Words from Our CEO


Since ancient times, sports has been a part of human culture. For billions of people around the globe, sports is a part of their daily life - a force in global culture that sustains through the conversations they have, and the content they consume.

It’s a powerful emotional connection fans have with their teams and players that tells us why sports market is estimated at $480-$620 billion, and growing faster than global GDP.

Sports is a big business, but it would be nothing without the fans supporting it. We know this because we’ve spent the last 12 months to understand sports fans and create a product that ensures their daily changing needs are served.

At Rooter, we believe in creating a new world of sports for fans and teams to engage, which over the next decade could change faster than the previous century.

Rooter aims to provide a definitive vision on the future sports fan, and how to best align with their needs.
