BLOG

The great sporting cathedrals, Nou Camp, Rod Laver Arena, Eden Gardens and Madison Square Garden, have hosted some of the biggest sporting histories. The reason they are written in the history books is because that night something special happened. The fans and the players became one and together they created magic. When nobody believed they could, they actually did. That is the power of sports. The idea of celebrating something together with like-minded individuals is what we at Rooter believe in.

At Rooter we create an atmosphere that makes an average sports enthusiast enjoy the game in a much more interactive manner. Rooter is the first sports social platform available on Android and iOS. Rooter aims to connect you to fans with similar sport interests around your workplace and home and also infinite number of sports fans around the world. You can log in with any of your social media profile, select your favorite team and players, and we will give you a “Passion Score”. Then you start looking for a sports fan based on the distance and Passion Score.

Rooter is a second screen app where we make it our mission to make sports a much needed break from our daily grind. Our dedicated tech and content team work hard day in day out to inculcate a culture of sports among our entire demographic. In this age of globalization where we are almost busy 24/7, 365 days, Rooter tries to bring everything live on your phone. Our mission is to popularize all sorts of sports in India. Since cricket is such a big sport in our country, we make it our aim to popularise and educate folks in other niche sports like Basketball, F1 etc.

At Rooter, we have an unique live match prediction game which let’s you enjoy the game in a much more interactive manner. You can compete with fellow rooters around you in this game which will help to increase your passion score. We have also introduced coins which acts as an incentive to participate and share and enjoy the beautiful game. Our live match prediction game also has a match forum where you can let your voice be heard and share your thoughts with thousands of rooters around you.

To test your knowledge we have pre match and weekly quizzes.

Currently we cover Football, Cricket, Tennis, Basketball and will be starting on F1 very soon.

Come Along. Cheer for your team and enjoy sports in a new way. Download Rooter.
